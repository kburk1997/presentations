Semester Timeline - Beginning

Introduction to RCOS

The first large group meeting will serve as an introduction to RCOS for new members, and will involve grading, the code of conduct, and other useful information.

Pitch Day

All projects, both returning and new, will give a 30 second to 1 minute pitch. This session will solely consist of these pitches.

Speed Dating

Speed dating will be introducing people who are looking for projects with projects who are looking for people. This session will only be for matching both together.

Semester Timeline - Middle

First Small Group

This will be the first small group meeting. Mentors will meet their mentees, and the project proposals and URP forms will be filled out.

Midterm Evaluations

Mentors will evaluate their mentees to ensure they are accomplishing (or at least attempting) the work they set out to do. Members will fill out an evaluation form.

Drop Deadline

Mentors should ensure that mentees are accomplishing things before this date.

HackRPI

HackRPI is RPI's annual hackathon. It will count as an RCOS bonus session.

Semester Timeline - End

Last Mandatory Small Group

This will be the last day your mentees need to show up to your small group.

Tentative Last Large Group

This will likely be the last large group meeting, provided there are no cancellations.

Make-Up Day

Depending on if there are any class cancellations, or if there happens to be an excess of presentations, this day will serve as an extra large group. Otherwise, it will be an optional small group.

Project Due Date

All work must be completed by 11:59 PM EST.
